Product description

The Ripple is a smart and minimalist speaker created by Jackson McConnell, designed exclusively for the hearing impaired. It is a highly thoughtful and revolutionary project, whereby the deaf people can enjoy listening to music without experiencing the sound part of it, but by feeling it instead.project focuses and uses the idea that the vibrations of music can stimulate and affect particular parts of a human brain, and how the normal brain perceives these waves. A similar experience is created by using the sound waves and their oscillatory motion effect.
